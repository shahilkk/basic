ACCESS STUDENT TALENT

DCU graduates continue to be highly sought after by employers, with a 97% employment rate according to the Graduate Outcomes Survey. DCU Business School ranks 19th in the world for graduate employment rate (QS World University Rankings), which is widely recognised as one of the biggest sources of international information on higher education ever compiled. Our graduates work in environments ranging from large multinationals to SMEs, family businesses and start-ups across every sector. Collaborate with our talented students by hosting a student project, providing placement opportunities, recruiting or delivering a guest lecture.

Several postgraduate programmes offer a Practicum component, whereby teams of Masters students work with a client company on a real business issue. As part of our on-going commitment to work with industry, we have several opportunities for client companies to engage with our postgraduate students in the Practicum process. This is intended to expose postgraduate students to a real-world problem to which they apply previously completed theoretical and conceptual work, and to further facilitate deep learning throughout the Masters.
